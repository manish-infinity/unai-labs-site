## OUR STORY

You've probably noticed that most "AI education" falls into two camps: deeply technical (for developers and researchers) or aggressively hyped (promising to 10x your productivity in 7 days).

Neither of these serves most people.

UnAI Labs exists for the person in between. The professional who wants to use AI thoughtfully. The lifelong learner who's curious about how their brain actually works. The person who suspects that the real advantage in an AI world isn't knowing every tool — it's knowing yourself.

We believe three things deeply:

**1. Neuroplasticity is the most underrated skill of our time.**
Your brain can change, adapt, and grow at any age. But it doesn't happen by accident — it happens through specific practices, environments, and habits that neuroscience has documented well. We teach those.

**2. AI literacy is a basic life skill now — not a technical specialty.**
You don't need to understand transformers or tokenization to use AI effectively. You need to understand how to prompt clearly, evaluate output critically, and integrate these tools into your actual work without losing your own judgment. That's what we teach.

**3. The brain and AI work best together when you understand both.**
This is our core insight: the people who will thrive aren't the ones with the most AI tools — they're the ones who understand their own cognition well enough to use AI as a genuine amplifier.

## WHAT WE TEACH

Our curriculum sits at the intersection of three bodies of knowledge:

**Neuroscience of Learning**
How memory consolidation works. Why spaced repetition beats cramming. How emotion, sleep, and stress affect your ability to retain and apply new information.

**Cognitive Performance**
Attention, focus, decision-making under uncertainty, and the habits that compound over time into meaningful cognitive advantages.

**AI Literacy and Practical Application**
How modern AI language models work (non-technically), how to write effective prompts, how to evaluate AI output for errors and bias, and how to build AI-assisted workflows that save time without creating dependency.

## OUR APPROACH

**Short, focused lessons.** Each module is designed to be completed in 20-30 minutes — because that's how attention and working memory function best.

**Science citations, not just claims.** When we say something works, we tell you where the research comes from.

**Practice built in.** Every lesson ends with a concrete exercise. Understanding without application doesn't create lasting change.

**No hype, no fear.** AI is genuinely useful. It's also genuinely limited. We give you an honest picture of both.
